Jon Bernthal

What makes a hero? A hero is someone that we look up to. Heroes embody what we truly value most. Therefore who we select as role models says a lot about who we are as people.

I admire Jon Bernthal for the following reasons.

  • He is a very brave man because of the values that he stands for on a daily basis
  • He has a podcast that outlines men's mental health and other problems that people have to deal with
  • The message that he displays is one of kindness and assistance
  • There are tons of people who look to him as a role model
  • He shows the world that dreams can be accomplished no matter what adversities lay ahead pf you

There are many values and attributes that Jon Bernthal instills in his audience which are admirable. I believe that having real conversations with real people who are struggling or who have been through horrific experiences is extremely important. Having a large following and backing that up with a genuine personality will always gain my respect.
